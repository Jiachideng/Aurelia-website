About

Hi, I'm Aurelia Leticia Tjokro, a junior Civil Engineering student at New York University. I'm passionate about learning, hardworking, driven, and always curious about new things. I have many hobbies, but photography and design are my top favorites. I love bringing ideas to life, whether it's capturing moments through photography, designing, or even building my own furniture. Seeing my ideas come to life is always exciting, and I'm always looking to learn more and grow, both in my studies and personal interests.
