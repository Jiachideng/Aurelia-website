PERMIAS Massachusetts Marketing Specialists



PERMIAS Massachusetts, part of a network of 80 such organizations across the United States, is a prominent non-profit run by Indonesian students. Our 2023/24 cabinet, comprised of 33 officers, serves a vibrant community of 525 members. We are dedicated to engaging Indonesian students in Massachusetts, fostering lifelong connections and showcasing our rich culture. Our events, frequently attended by over 100 Indonesian students, range from cultural showcases to educational workshops led by officers with diverse backgrounds.

As a marketing specialist, photographer, and officer, I oversee our Instagram, creating diverse content for significant events such as webinar orientations, fundraising, and large gatherings like Lions Club Parade, Bean Town, etc. Furthermore, my design and sale of merchandise, including 300 t-shirts and 150 hoodies, contributed to a revenue of approximately $4680 USD in a month.
